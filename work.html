At the Beach

Music Video

This video won 2012's Best Video at the Radio Milwaukee Music Awards. Uncle Larry frontman, Bill Baldus, is a do-it-all creative genius, making his own props, building sets, writing music, choreography and acting. Whenever Bill has a project, I try to be involved. Here I am an anemone dancer, but in other videos, I helped with props, wardrobe, makeup, puppeteering, and even briefly, manning the camera from the top of a 12 foot ladder.

Until Death

Short Film

We were challenged to make a film of the following: Genre - Horror; Character - Daniel Carson, Telepathy Expert; Prop - A box; Line - "Nevermind, I'll ask someone else." and 48 hours to go from blank page to a final film.

The film also won Best Special Effects Worldwide at Filmapalooza in Los Angeles. It beat out 4,022 films from 133 cities across the globe! My role was Art Director.

88Nine Radio Milwaukee App

iOS App

88Nine Radio Milwaukee asked us to update their app to be more fully featured. For V2 of the app, the DJs wanted to ability to favorite and purchase songs, see events and save them to your calendar and listen to On Demand programs. I received wireframes to work off, but I had some usability concerns. So I convinced the client to let me rework the UX as I executed visual design. When it debuted, it had a 5 star rating on the App Store and today has over 4,000 active users.
