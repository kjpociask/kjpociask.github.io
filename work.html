Spreenkler Rebrand

Branding and Identity

Spreenkler was a creative agency whose focus shifted as they matured. The unique model originally focused on training and community, but they grew into software engineering with a user-centered design approach. Many didn't understand the company evolution. The need for a rebrand was clear. I served as Brand Strategist, Art Director and Designer on this project.

Artís

Web and Video

Artís is a coffee shop and roastery based in San Francisco. They came to us to build their web and video presence. I served as Creative Director, doing everything from brand strategy to script writing, from moodboarding to reviewing video edits.

Proxy

iOS App

Proxy encourages kids to play together in real life by completing missions of social good. The Proxy Pal, a tagalong pal that is equipped with NFC, records their meetings and efforts in the Proxy App. In doing so, it creates a safe social network for kids. My role in this project was User Experience and Visual Designer.

Beauty App

Concept iOS App

This concept app was designed to disrupt and innovate shopping for cosmetics. My role was Art Director.

Yell and Tell

Public Service Announcement

Yell and Tell is a non-profit organization that teaches kids what to do in dangerous situations. This PSA was made on a shoestring budget, yet still managed to capture attention of the community. Marcus Theaters liked the cause and agreed to play it before kids movies and in lobbies. My role was Creative Director and Art Director (film).

88Nine Radio Milwaukee App

iOS App

88Nine Radio Milwaukee asked us to update their app to be more fully featured. For V2 of the app, the DJs wanted to ability to favorite and purchase songs, see events and save them to your calendar and listen to On Demand programs. I received wireframes to work off, but I had some usability concerns. So I convinced the client to let me rework the UX as I executed visual design. When it debuted, it had a 5 star rating on the App Store and today has over 4,000 active users. My role was User Experience and Visual Designer and Art Director.

At the Beach

Music Video

This Uncle Larry music video won 2012's Best Video at the Radio Milwaukee Music Awards. My role was Actor and Anemone Dancer.

Uncle Larry frontman, Bill Baldus, is a do-it-all creative genius, making his own props, building sets, writing music, choreography and acting. I try to be involved whenever he has a project. I have helped with props, wardrobe, makeup, puppeteering, and manning the camera from the top of a 12' ladder.

Until Death

Short Film

This won Best Film in the 48 Hour Film Competition. It also won Best Special Effects Worldwide at Filmapalooza in Los Angeles. It beat out 4,022 films from 133 cities across the globe! My role was Art Director.

We had 48 hours to go from blank page to a final film with the following:

  • Genre - Horror
  • Character - Daniel Carson, Telepathy Expert
  • Prop - A box
  • Line - "Nevermind, I'll ask someone else."
